Introduction: Bangladesh is a riverine country. In this fast-moving modern age, a journey by boat offers a different taste in communication. It brings us closer to the beauty of nature.

Occasion/Planning: It was an autumn vacation. I enjoyed a journey by boat from Kotwalipara to Gopalgonj. Two friends were with me.

Arrangement: For the journey, we hired a boat. There were two boatmen. We took clothes, food, and a camera with us.

Beginning of the journey: It was 13 February 2025. The day was bright. The current and the wind were in our favor. The boatmen hoisted the sail.
